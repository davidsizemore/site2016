The Ventriloquist

The Ventriloquist was an underground student newspaper started by my brother and a few of his friends at their university. It offered an alternative voice on a campus with an oppressively one-note religious culture. The paper was supported by Campus Progress, a national organization that works with and for young people to promote progressive solutions to key political and social challenges.

Five years later, long after my brother graduated and moved on, distribution of the paper was forcibly shut down by the school's administration, who literally ran around removing stacks of papers from students' arms. Pretty amazing.

I was proud to help create their visual identity, and create spot illustrations and layouts for their first few issues.
