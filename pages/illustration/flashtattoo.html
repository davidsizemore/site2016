Flash Tattoo

A series of flash tattoo designs for bloggers. They were handed out on buttons at the MailChimp ALT Conference party, where we teamed up with Tattly to create a tattoo parlor.

I got the chance to help design the space for our parlor, and headed out to Salt Lake City to set everything up. I even took a short stint as a tattooist, applying Tattlys to conference attendees.
