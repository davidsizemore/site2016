MailChimp's Features page is one of the more important pages on the site. You hit the home page, check out the features for a bit, see what the prices look like, and, hopefully, people give us loads of money to use our product. Or sign up for free.

We wanted to create an awesome image to kick off the page, and decided to use food, to go along with the theme we developed for the example campaign company, Nom Depot.

Fun fact! Fish smells bad if you leave it out and shine lights on it. This is our original photo, and the final image after waaay too much time in Photoshop.

Our local alt weekly newspaper was coming by to interview our CEO, and took pictures of our shoot. My mom was proud/confused.

We knew fish was on the menu, but it took lot of shooting and grocery shopping to lock down the other props.

Unfortunately for us, most of the really tasty stuff doesn't do well unfridgerated for long periods of time. I don't think we ever touched the cheese.

But the artisinal Coke was *delicious.*
