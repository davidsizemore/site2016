David Sizemore Design

David Sizemore is a designer living in Atlanta. He works as an art director for MailChimp.

Before drinking the technology startup kool-aid, David billed agency hours for brands like Microsoft, Russell Athletics, Goodyear, and Carnival. He continues to participate in freelance branding and illustration projects, and is available to speak at design gatherings and bar mitzvahs.
