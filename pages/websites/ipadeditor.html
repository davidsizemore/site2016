MC Editor is a pretty neat app developed by our mobile team. It allowed teams to sync their campaigns to their iPads and work on them offline. The drag-and-drop editor, adding photos, and commenting features were all there, whether you were connected to the internet or not. It was a big deal, and we wanted to give it a little more attention than our other pages for other apps.

When my work involves more than just designing styles or a layout, I like to mock up the interactions. It helps me and the devs talk through my ideas, and keeps me from saying things like "And then this part would come in from there like *shwing* and that would *tick tick tick* you know?"

Those conversations are so valuable in evolving the site into something that works well. Development and design are back-and-forth processes at MC, and in the end, we normally have something we're all happy with. Even if it scroll jacks. I'm sorry.
