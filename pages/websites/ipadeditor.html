MailChimp Editor Site

MC Editor was a neat app developed by the MailChimp mobile team. It allowed users to sync their campaigns to an iPad and work offline. The drag-and-drop editor, adding photos, and commenting features were all there, whether you were connected to the internet or not. It was a big deal, and we wanted to give it a little more marketing attention. Alas, MC Editor now lives in the app graveyard, as does this site.
